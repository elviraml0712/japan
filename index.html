Japan

Osaka is a large port city and commercial center on the Japanese island of Honshu. It's known for its modern architecture, nightlife and hearty street food.

Tokyo, Japan’s busy capital, mixes the ultramodern and the traditional. The opulent Meiji Shinto Shrine is known for its towering gate and surrounding woods.

Kyoto, is a city on the island of Honshu. It's famous for its classical Buddhist temples, Shinto shrines and traditional wooden houses.

Hiroshima, a modern city on Japan’s Honshu Island. Today, Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park commemorates the 1945 event.
